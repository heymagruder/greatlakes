Inspections

We Inspect:
  • Skylights and all other roof penetrations.
  • Siding installation and condition.
  • orches, decks, steps, handrails, and walkways.
  • Drainage and grading.
  • Garage door and safety sensor functionality.
  • Insulation and ventilation.
  • Electrical service line and meter box.
  • Panels, fuses, and breakers.
  • Grounding and bonding.
  • Heating and cooling systems
  • Interior plumbing and faucet fixtures.
  • Water heating system.
  • Basements, crawl spaces, and foundations.
  • Sump pumps.
  • Water penetration into foundation or crawlspace.
  • Foundation settling or movement.

Additional Services

Septic system inspection and flow testing.

Septic systems are an often overlooked, yet integral, part of many homes. Since septic tank and field replacement can be costly, Great Lakes Home Inspectors, LLC suggests an additional inspection and flow test to make sure the system that is currently installed is operating properly, without any leaks or other safety and functionality issues.

Radon air testing.

Radon is a tasteless, odorless, naturally occurring gas, which (at elevated levels) can cause severely negative health effects; radon is said to be the number one cause of lung cancer in nonsmokers in the United States. Great Lakes Home Inspectors, LLC works closely with a certified, independent lab to complete radon testing and get results as quickly as possible.

Surface mold testing

There are many different types of mold in the environment that can negatively impact health. With Great Lakes Home Inspectors, LLC, any visible staining or “mold like substances” will be documented in our report as part of our basic home inspection service package. Additionally, we can work with a certified, independent lab to have any suspected mold professionally analyzed, if desired by the client.

Well water quality testing

Samples are collected and sent to a certified, third-party lab to run an analysis on water quality. This test will check for the presence and levels of the following: E. coli, total coliform bacteria, nitrate, nitrite, lead, and arsenic.

Several other lab-based tests are available up on client request; we are happy to discuss those options in person or over the phone with the client.
